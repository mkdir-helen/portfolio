About Me

While trying to brush up on my Photoshop skills, I came across courses on how to code websites. I decided to give learning to build websites a try and I instantly fell in love with the world of coding. The coding language gives me the kind of satisfaction similar to when you finish fitting the puzzle pieces together, except it's different in that there is an unlimited supply of puzzle pieces; it's up to your creative instinct as to how to organize or pick the right pieces--whether it's trending or fits "just right."
